Winter

Well, it does get chilling sometimes, doesn't it. I went for a walk the other day. I put on my best wooly hat and scarf. But then it got really windy and the wind blew off my hat! Can you believe it? Well, happily I did manage to retrieve it with the help of a kind gentleman who was also chasing his own hat. I made sure I said thank you and then we had a little chat about the weather. The forecast had predicted that it was going to get really cold and even windier! And it did. So I was much more careful the next time.
